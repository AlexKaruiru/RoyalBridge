Intro

Royal Bridge Empire Modeling Agency is an entity that focuses on nurturing and mentoring young talents towards building a brighter future through art. The agency was established in September 2020 and prides itself to have mentored in excess of 30 youth. We are currently based in Ongata Rongai with the vision of going international. Our core services include modeling, fashion design, beauty and related marketing. Starting a modeling career can be scary at first. This is an industry that is highly driven by physical standards of beauty. Modeling is a career and therefore a lot of work is involved. Many established models attest that they learned on the job, but what happens when no one gives you the opportunity of a first job? What do you do when you keep getting turned down at modeling auditions? The answer is simple, learn and build your skill..

ACCOUNTABILITY- Our self critical approach to tasks ensures that we continuously achieve client satisfaction. INTEGRITY- Our team ensures the same level of commitment and execution every time, with no supervision. CATWALK CLASS Our vision as an agency is to equip models with the necessary skills and knowledge to ensure they can compete favorably with models anywhere in the world. All models signed by Royal Bridge Empire Modelling agency,get 6 months of catwalk training each year. Unfortunately, we cannot sign every model in Kenya, hence, we open our doors to aspiring models who wish to get the necessary training to start their modelling journey. Our training takes place in the following months: March to May- Session 1 August to October- Session 2 We train once every week, Sunday afternoons, from 2pm to 5pm..

About

Royal Bridge is a Modelling agecy working country wide with 51+ Models. We are working with top designers and clients and also hunt new faces to represent us in National and International Pageants. We are looking for good models who are looking to work anywhere in the country with us. We enjoy extremely high levels of client satisfaction. We offer total modeling and events experience to our clients to explore different parts of the country's beauties. We are working in Nairobi, Kiambu, Kajiado, Neru, and Other counties. We always promote new and deserving candidates. Who wants to work can apply in our casting call and we have social for models where you can post anything to interact with other artists and clients.
